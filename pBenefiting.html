Benefits of PMPDP
  • Hydro power will increase renewable energy an enhance Ghana's climate change commitment under the United Nations Convention on Climate Change.

  • Power infrastructure will increase access to electricity in the Northern part of Ghana by 90%.

  • Improve voltage and reliability of Power supply to NEDCo;

  • Reduce the frequency of floods downstream and avert the avert the destruction of lives and property.

  • Facilitate the development of 20,000 hectare irrigation scheme, the largest in the country and boost agriculture and other economic activities in the Upper and North East regions.
  • Potential for smaller irrigation schemes around the reservoir.

  • Create jobs and increase livelihoods.
